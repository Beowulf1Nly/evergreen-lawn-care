Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about pricing, billing, and how it works.

Why is the first mow only $19? ＋
It's our way of letting you try us risk-free. We're confident you'll love the results — so we make it easy to say yes. After your first visit, you're billed your regular plan rate automatically.
What happens after my first mow? ＋
Your card is automatically charged after each mow — bi-weekly customers are charged per visit at their bi-weekly rate, and weekly customers are charged per visit at their weekly rate.
How is my price calculated? ＋
We estimate your yard size from your address and show you a price instantly — just a fair, transparent quote in seconds. If the price doesn't look right, give us a call at (352) 462-0627 and we'll sort it out.
Can I cancel my plan? ＋
Absolutely — no contracts, no cancellation fees. You can cancel directly from your customer profile, or just text or call us at (352) 462-0627 anytime and we'll handle it right away.
Is my card information secure? ＋
100%. All payments are processed through Stripe, which is PCI Level 1 certified — the highest standard in payment security. We never see or store your card number.
Do I need to be home? ＋
Nope! You don't need to be home for us to mow. Just make sure we have access to your yard — if there's a gate, leave it unlocked or drop the code in your booking notes. We'll take care of the rest.
Do you serve my area? ＋
We currently serve all of Citrus County, FL — including Crystal River, Inverness, Lecanto, Homosassa, Citrus Springs, Floral City, and Beverly Hills. Enter your address in the quote form and we'll confirm right away.
