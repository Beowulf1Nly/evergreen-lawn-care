Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking and paying.

Do I have to pay before you mow? ＋
Nope. By default we work on a pay-after-service model — you book, we show up and mow, then we send you an invoice by email afterward. No credit card required to schedule.
Can I pay upfront or set up auto-pay? ＋
Yes! When you book, you can flip the toggle to Pay Now / Auto-Pay. For one-time visits you'll be taken to a secure Stripe checkout to pay immediately. For recurring plans (bi-weekly or weekly), you can subscribe and your card gets charged automatically each billing period — no manual invoice, no extra steps.
How does the invoice work after the job? ＋
Once we finish your lawn, we send a professional invoice to the email you provided. It'll have a Pay Now button powered by Stripe — you can pay securely from any phone or computer in under a minute. Invoices are due within 7 days.
What payment methods are accepted? ＋
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) through Stripe's secure checkout. Cash is also welcome — just let us know in the booking notes.
Can I cancel or pause my recurring plan? ＋
Absolutely — no contracts, no cancellation fees. Just text or call us at (352) 462-0627 and we'll sort it out right away.
Is my payment info secure? ＋
100%. All payments go through Stripe, which is PCI Level 1 certified — the highest standard for payment security. We never see or store your card details.
